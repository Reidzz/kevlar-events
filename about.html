Kevlar's Mission

Kevlar Events is an Events Management company based in Burntisland. Kevlar Events have one main USP and that is security. The brand name itself has connotations with safety and strength. Kevlar Events ensure attendees feel comfortable in the venue via reliable and trained security staff and procedures.


Kevlar Events aim to establish an annual open-air music event/festival in Fife and hybrid music-sporting events across Scotland. This includes but is not limited to downhill MTB, skateboard and motocross.


2020 has been a tough and challenging year for everyone. Significant research on post-covid events has informed our health and safety practices to ensure our events are hygienic and safe to attend. The health and safety of our attendees is our highest priority.


Please be advised that our core business is large scale events. From hosting residential events, we learned that the logistical effort required for each event is difficult to maintain weekly. The price Fifer's want to pay is also far from the price we set. Ticket sales and setting up the equipment once is far less effort and more profitable.


However, we love to party and making events special for clients. If you have an upcoming event and you would like a quote, please send us an enquiry and we will get back to you shortly.
