Kevlar's Mission

Kevlar Events is an independent business based in Burntisland that is dedicated to providing high class music and sporting events within Scotland.


Founded by Bryce Methven in 2019, Kevlar has hosted many parties around Scotland and is planning to branch out into extreme sporting events in the future.


2020 has been a tough and challenging year for everyone, including all of us at Kevlar. However, this has not stopped us from achieving our mission to provide the best and highest quality events for all of our clients throughout the year.


Kevlar has been researching and adapting health and safety measures to ensure our events are completely safe and covid free. The health and safety of our clients is Kevlar's highest priority.


As restrictions in Scotland are being lifted we are now taking bookings for events throughout the remainder of 2021. Get in touch with us if you would like Kevlar to host your next event or if you have any questions at all regarding our services. We look forward to hearing from you!
